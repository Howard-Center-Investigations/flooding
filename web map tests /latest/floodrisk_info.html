Flood Risk Areas


The flood zone data is calculated by FEMA to evaluate area's risk for flooding.

Several calculations based on weather data have been considered. The latest calculations are based on 2016 data.

We only show flood zones with at least 1% risk.

Areas with a 1% annual chance of flooding and a 26% chance of flooding over the life of a 30‐year mortgage.

Areas with 0.02 percent annual chance of moderate flood hazard, usually the area between the limits of the 100‐ year and 500‐year floods.

The data was obtained through DC Flood Risk Tool and OPEN DATA DC
